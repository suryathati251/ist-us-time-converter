⚡ Daylight Saving Time

US clocks spring forward 1 hr on the 2nd Sunday in March and fall back on the 1st Sunday in November. During DST: EST→EDT (UTC−4), CST→CDT (UTC−5), PST→PDT (UTC−7). India (IST) never observes DST — always UTC+5:30.
