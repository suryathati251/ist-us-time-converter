⚡ Daylight Saving Time (DST)

US clocks spring forward 1 hour on the 2nd Sunday of March and fall back on the 1st Sunday of November. During DST: EST→EDT (UTC−4), CST→CDT (UTC−5), PST→PDT (UTC−7). India (IST) never observes DST and stays at UTC+5:30 all year. The converter auto-detects DST for any date you enter.
